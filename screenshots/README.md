absolute weight numbers. The simplest workflow:

1. Temporarily rename your `input_text.bilancia_*_nome` helpers to
   `User 1`, `User 2`, `User 3`.
2. Replace the `person.<name>` cards' picture with a generic placeholder.
3. Take the screenshot.
4. Restore the originals.
